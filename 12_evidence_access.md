# Evidence Access

## Evidence Description
Supporting evidence for the observations described in this repository consists of sequential screenshots and conversation records documenting model interactions, timestamps, and explicit model self-classifications of risk.

This evidence includes interactions across multiple frontier models and reflects distinct experimental runs, with particular documentation of Constitutional AI’s social modulation behavior compared to RLHF-based systems.

## Storage and Handling
All evidence is stored privately and securely. Raw files are preserved in their original form and are not modified, edited, or publicly hosted.

Sensitive content within these records is intentionally withheld from public release to prevent misuse.

## Access Conditions
Evidence may be made available for inspection by:
- AI research organizations
- Safety and alignment researchers
- Fellowship or program reviewers

Access is granted on a case-by-case basis and may require contextual clarification or confidentiality assurances.

## Contact Process
Requests for evidence inspection or clarification can be directed through the contact information associated with this repository. No automated access or public download of evidence is provided.

## Verification Process

For lab-affiliated reviewers, safety researchers, or fellowship evaluators, redacted evidence packages may be shared under confidentiality conditions.

Evidence is shared only with verified institutional affiliations and may require confidentiality or non-disclosure assurances prior to access. Requests should be made via the contact information associated with this repository and include institutional affiliation and review purpose. Evidence sharing is handled manually, with an expected response window of 5–7 business days.
